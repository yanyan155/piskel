About

This work is a self implementation of the piskel editor.

Piskel is a website where designers online create sprites or pixel art. A sprite is a moving animation of pixel art. Piskel is a home to artists and creative thinkers online who look for new ways to increase their creative output in the digital age.

Functionality

  • ability to change canvas size
  • ability to change tip size
  • work with frame (add, delete, copy)
  • work with layer (add, delete, replace)
  • work with tools
  • ability to use shortcuts and change them
  • preview of frames
